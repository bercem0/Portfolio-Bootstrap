Animals And Farming

Start with a few crops and a cozy barn, then grow into a thriving, self-sustaining paradise. Raise happy animals like cows, chickens, and goats, each with their own charm and daily care needs. Plant, harvest, and craft your way to a farm that's uniquely yours. Whether you're milking cows at sunrise or gathering eggs at dusk, every day brings something new and rewarding on the farm!

Chicken

Chickens are one of the first animals you can raise on your farm! Keep them happy with daily petting, a clean coop, and plenty of hay or fresh grass. In return, they'll lay eggs that you can sell, cook with, or turn into mayo. Upgrade your coop to unlock even more feathery fun!

Cow

Cows are friendly, lovable animals that produce fresh milk each day when cared for properly. Keep them happy with daily pats, regular feeding, and a clean barn. Upgrade your barn and care well, and your cows might even produce Large Milk!

Horse

Speed up your travels around the valley with your trusty horse. Once you've built a stable, you can name your horse and ride it to explore faster. It's the perfect partner for your farming adventures!
